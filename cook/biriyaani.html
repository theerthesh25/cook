BIRIYANI

Certainly! Here's a basic recipe for making Chicken Biryani. This recipe serves 4-6 people.


Ingredients:


For Marinating the Chicken:

500 grams (about 1.1 pounds) chicken, cut into pieces
1 cup plain yogurt
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon red chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon garam masala
Salt to taste

For the Rice:

2 cups long-grain basmati rice
Water for cooking rice
2-3 green cardamom pods
2-3 cloves
1-inch cinnamon stick
Salt to taste

For Biryani Assembly:

2 large onions
thinly sliced 2 tomatoes
chopped 2-3 green chilies
slit 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves
2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
A pinch of saffron strands soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk 1/4 cup fried onions (for garnish)

Steps

Marinate the Chicken

1) In a bowl, combine the yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
2) Add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat them evenly with the marinade.
3) Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes or refrigerate for a few hours for better flavor.

.

Cook the Rice


4) Wash the basmati rice thoroughly until the water runs clear. Drain.
5) In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the washed rice, green cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and salt.
6) Cook the rice until it's 70-80% done.
7) It should still have a slight bite to it.
8) Drain the rice and set it aside.

Prepare Fried Onions

9)Heat oil in a pan and fry the thinly sliced onions until they turn golden brown. Remove and set aside.
10) Assemble the Biryani:In a large, heavy-bottomed pan or a biryani pot, add the marinated chicken and spread it evenly.
11) Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken.
12) Sprinkle chopped mint leaves, chopped coriander leaves, and green chilies on top.
13) Drizzle ghee over the rice.
14) Sprinkle the saffron-infused milk on top.
15) Finally, sprinkle the fried onions over the rice.


Dum Cooking

16) Cover the biryani pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough to trap steam.
17) Cook on low heat (sim) for about 30-40 minutes. This slow cooking process is known as "dum cooking." It allows the flavors to meld together.


Serve

18) Once the biryani is cooked, gently fluff the rice and mix it with the chicken and spices. Serve hot with raita (yogurt dip) or a side salad.
19) Enjoy your homemade Chicken Biryani!
