Caprese Salad

Certainly! Caprese Salad is a classic Italian dish that's incredibly easy to make and showcases the delicious combination of fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and fragrant basil.

Here's how to prepare a basic Caprese Salad


Ingredients


2 large ripe tomatoes
8 ounces (about 225 grams) fresh mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves
Extra-virgin olive oil
Balsamic vinegar (optional)
Salt and freshly ground black pepper


Instructions


Slice the Tomatoes and Mozzarella


Wash and dry the tomatoes and basil leaves.
Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into equal-sized, approximately 1/4-inch (0.5 cm) thick rounds.


Arrange the Caprese Salad


On a serving platter or individual plates, arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices alternately, overlapping them slightly.
This creates a visually appealing pattern.

Layer with Basil Leaves

Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
You can use whole leaves or tear them into smaller pieces, depending on your preference.

Drizzle with Olive Oil

Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil generously over the tomato and mozzarella slices.
This adds flavor and richness to the salad.

Season with Salt and Pepper

Sprinkle a pinch of salt and some freshly ground black pepper over the salad to enhance the flavors.
Be cautious with the salt, as the mozzarella can be a bit salty.

Optional Balsamic Glaze

If you like, you can drizzle a small amount of balsamic vinegar or balsamic glaze over the Caprese Salad.
This adds a sweet and tangy flavor.
Use it sparingly, as a little goes a long way.

Serve Fresh

Serve the Caprese Salad immediately while the ingredients are fresh and the flavors are vibrant.
Caprese Salad is perfect as a light appetizer or side dish and is best enjoyed during the summer months when tomatoes and basil are at their peak.
It's a delightful dish that celebrates the simplicity of fresh ingredients and the essence of Italian cuisine.
