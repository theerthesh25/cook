CHICKEN CURRY

Certainly! Here's a basic recipe for making Chicken Curry. This recipe serves 4-6 people.

Ingredients

500 grams (about 1.1 pounds) chicken, cut into pieces
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 large onion, finely chopped
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
1-inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
2 tomatoes, chopped
2-3 green chilies, slit (adjust to your spice preference)
1/2 cup plain yogurt
1 teaspoon cumin powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to your spice preference)
1 teaspoon garam masala
Salt to taste
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps


Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes. You can marinate it for a longer time for better flavor if you wish.

Heat Oil

Heat vegetable oil in a large, heavy-bottomed pan or pot over medium heat.

Sauté Onions

Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent and start to turn golden brown.

Add Ginger and Garlic

Add the minced garlic and grated ginger.
Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.

Add Spices

Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala.
Sauté for a minute or two to toast the spices and release their flavors.

Add Tomatoes

Add the chopped tomatoes and green chilies.
Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.

Cook the Chicken

Add the marinated chicken along with the marinade into the pan.
Stir well to coat the chicken with the spice mixture

Simmer

Cover the pan and let the chicken simmer on low to medium heat for about 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

Adjust Seasoning

Taste the curry and adjust the salt and spice levels according to your preference.
You can add more red chili powder for extra heat if desired.

Garnish and Serve

Once the chicken is cooked and the curry reaches your desired consistency, garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Serve

Chicken curry can be served with rice, naan, or roti (Indian flatbread).
Enjoy your homemade Chicken Curry!
